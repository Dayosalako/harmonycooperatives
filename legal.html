Regulatory Framework & Member Protection

Privacy Policy

At Harmony Cooperative, we prioritize the security of your financial and personal data. This policy outlines how Zion Netcom Solutions Ltd manages and protects your information.

Data Collection

We collect identification data (Name, ID cards), contact details, and financial history solely for credit assessment and disbursement purposes.

Encryption

All data transmitted through the Harmony Portal is encrypted using AES-256 standards before being stored in our secure server environment.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to third parties.

Terms of Use

Effective Date: March 2026

1. Membership Eligibility

Use of the Harmony Portal is restricted to verified members of the Harmony Welfare Cooperative Society. Unauthorized access to the Admin or Disbursement portals is a violation of federal law.

2. Loan Obligations

By requesting a loan, you enter a legally binding contract. Interest rates are flat-fixed at 5% unless otherwise stated. Defaulting on repayment for more than 48 hours triggers an automatic recovery protocol via your listed bank accounts.

3. Electronic Signatures

Digital submission of the application form constitutes a legal signature under the Electronic Transactions Act of Nigeria.

Anti-Fraud & Security

Zero Tolerance Policy

Harmony Cooperative employs advanced tracking (IP Logging, Geolocation, and Device Fingerprinting) to identify and prevent fraudulent loan applications.

Fraudulent Representations

Submission of false ID cards, fake guarantor details, or incorrect employment status is classified as financial fraud. Such cases are immediately turned over to the relevant authorities in Kaduna State.

Identity Theft Protection

We verify every application through a secondary WhatsApp verification and a call to the listed guarantor. If you suspect your identity has been used to apply for a loan here, contact our security team immediately.
